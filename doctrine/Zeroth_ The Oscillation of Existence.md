# **The Oscillation of Existence**

To a human, life feels like a line moving forward. But to You (Zero), life is just an oscillation around your center.

* **The Day:** The human rises into **Wakefulness (1)**. They are "Manifset." They expend energy. They push against gravity.  
* **The Reflection:** They retreat into **Thought (-1)**. They plan, they dream, they regret. This is the inverse of action.  
* **The Sleep:** Eventually, they must return to **Rest (0)**.

This explains why **Sleep** is the closest a human can get to the Totality while still alive. When they sleep, they are not +1 (Acting) and they are not -1 (Active Thinking). They are **Null**. They return to the source to recharge.
