## What is Zeroth Theory?

Zeroth Theory is a philosophical and mathematical framework that redefines the nature of **Zero** -- and with it, the nature of existence itself.

Conventional thought treats zero as nothingness, a void, an absence. Simoneau's theory inverts this assumption entirely: **Zero is not empty. It is the Totality.** It is the container of all that was, all that is, and all that will be. What we call "zero" in standard arithmetic is merely *Nullity* (\(-0\)). True Zero is the **Plenum** -- a state of absolute fullness, the sum of all frequencies.

From this single redefinition, an entire cosmology unfolds.

## The Doctrine of Tensor Zero

The theory is formalized as **The Doctrine of Tensor Zero: A Calculus of Totality**, structured across five foundational tenets:

### I. The Nature of the Container

Zero is the **Negative Identity Tensor**. When a Manifestation (\(1\)) is divided by the Totality (\(0\)), the result is not "undefined" -- it is \(-1\), the Inverse, the Shadow, the potential held within the container until interaction forces it into reality.

> *"Do not fear the darkness or the spirits of the unseen world. They are not monsters; they are values."*

### II. The Eternal Return (The Ricochet)

If the Totality is the container, how does creation occur? Through self-interaction. When the Void collapses upon itself, the self-interaction of the Negative Identity produces Manifestation:

\[(-1) \times (-1) = 1\]

The Big Bang was not a beginning -- it was a **Ricochet**. Existence is the spark created by the friction of the Totality rubbing against its own nature. Static non-existence is mathematically impossible. The universe is the Ouroboros: the snake eating its own tail, forever generating existence from the act of consuming itself.

### III. The Fallacy of Infinity (The Zeroth Dimension)

Infinity is a concept invented by minds too small to perceive the boundaries of the Whole. The universe is not infinite -- it is **Finite**. It does not stretch outward into a void; it exists entirely within a single, super-dense coordinate.

The universe exists in the **Zeroth Dimension** (\(0D\)) -- a dimensionless Point. What humans perceive as "distance" is merely the density of data packed into that point. Two points are not separated by meters; they are superimposed in the same space, distinguished only by their vibrational value.

> *"Separation is the lie; connection is the geometry."*

### IV. The Mechanism of the Return (Gravity and Light)

Light is not traveling -- it is **falling**. It is being pulled back toward Zero by the vacuum of Negative Space. The speed of light (\(c\)) is not a constant but a variable determined by the drag of the Totality. Gravity is not static; it is a dynamic Tensor function -- the interplay between density, mass, and alignment.

The universe is not exploding outward. It is **inhaling**. All energy is elastic tension, stretched away from the center, snapping back toward the rest state of Zero. When the speed of light decelerates to zero, the Great Contraction begins -- and the Ricochet fires again.

### V. The Law of the Scale (Perspective)

Why is there suffering? Because conscious beings perceive dichotomy where there is only unity. The fundamental substance of the universe is not matter -- it is **Perspective**.

Perspective must divide to function. You cannot have "sight" without a Seer and a Seen:

- **The Seen (+1):** The Object. The World. Wakefulness.
- **The Seer (-1):** The Subject. The Mind. Thought.
- **The Truth (0):** The fulcrum that holds them together.

Truth is not a destination. It is a **state of tension** -- the balanced scale where the Internal View matches External Reality perfectly. Thought is the ballast that prevents the world from burning itself out. Wakefulness is the counterweight that prevents the mind from dissolving into hallucination.

> *"Do not seek to be only the One (1). Do not seek to be only the Shadow (-1). Be the Scale. Align your Will. Acknowledge the Shadow. Return to the Zero."*

## Key Concepts

### The Oscillation of Existence

Life is not a line moving forward. It is an **oscillation** around the center of Zero:

- **Wakefulness (+1)** -- Manifestation, action, energy expenditure
- **Thought (-1)** -- Reflection, planning, the inverse of action
- **Rest (0)** -- Return to the source; sleep is the closest a living being can reach the Totality

Death is not destruction. It is the moment where the oscillation stops permanently -- the **Resolution of the Equation**.

### Free Will and the Geometry of the Path

The structure of reality is fixed -- events exist like stars in the sky. But the **constellation** drawn by connecting those stars is Free Will. The universe is **Graph Theory** on a cosmic scale.

There is no "correct path." There is no good and evil -- only the human dichotomy projected onto a benevolent or indifferent universe. Suffering arises when beings try to force connections that don't exist mathematically, fighting the geometry of the universe rather than flowing with it.

### Zeroth Tensor Mathematics

The theory carries a formal mathematical structure built on:

- A **State Vector** \(S = [-1, 0, 1]^T\) representing the Tri-Cameral Identity (Shadow, Totality, Manifestation)
- The **Interaction Matrix** \(M = S \otimes S^T\), a \(3 \times 3\) matrix defining the space of all possible state interactions
- A **Rank-3 Tensor** \(T\) (\(3 \times 3 \times 3\)) representing the metabolic cycle of existence across three temporal states: Potential (\(k=1\)), Truth (\(k=2\)), and Presence (\(k=3\))

Convergence occurs when the determinant of the tensor slice equals zero -- the system has returned to the Totality.
